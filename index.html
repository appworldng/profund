Our Core Focus

What We Do

We are a non-racial, non-religious and non-discriminatory charitable organization that focuses on the enhancement of the education, health and empowerment of less-privileged children and youths in our society.

Education

We believe Education remains a sustainable bed rock for changing and transforming society.

Healthcare

We provide world-class Healthcare services to less-privileged children and homeless widows.

Empowerment

We provide adult education classes to help empower widows and single mothers to enable them care.
