About Us

In Loving Memory of Daniel...

Daniel Ogechi Akujobi Memorial (DOAM) Foundation is a charitable, humanitarian, and non-governmental organization established in 2006, in memory of Master Daniel Ogechi Akujobi (1992-2005) who died from injuries he sustained in a fatal vehicle accident on his way back to school along the Lagos-Ibadan expressway, Ogun State Nigeria.

He was the only son of his parents.

Who We Are

We are the voice to the less privileged...

Our Foundation is a non-racial, no-religious and a non-discriminatory organization. We respect and work with all manners of people, no matter where you come from or where you were born.

Our targeted program beneficiaries include:

  • Orphans and Vulnerable Children (OVC)
  • Less privileged families
  • Less privileged communities

Achievements

And, what have we done so far???

Since inception, we have offered scholarship to a number of vulnerable but intelligent children in primary, secondary and tertiary institutions. We've also executed other life improving programs such as de-worming of children, mentorship, educational, nutritional and psycho-social support to children and youths in the society and skill acquisition training programs for women and provided takeoff grants for their respective trades.

We built and equipped the DOAMF Accident & Trauma Centre located along the Lagos-Ibadan Expressway, procured an ambulance to support lifesaving activities at the Centre, refurbished eight secondary school libraries, five sickbays, organized  free community healthcare programs, among other numerous projects.  Over 60,000 lives and counting have been touched by our programs.
